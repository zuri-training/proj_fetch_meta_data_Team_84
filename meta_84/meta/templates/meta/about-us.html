Our story

84Meta Extractor is a metadata extraction company that helps businesses discover, analyze and utilize their data. We specialize in extracting information from documents and databases for use in your business.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use, flexible metadata extraction service for content owners and publishers. We are committed to ensuring our users get accurate metadata of their uploaded files at all time.

At 84Meta, we believe that metadata is the most important part of any business. Our team is dedicated to making sure that you have all of the information you need and that it's as good as it can be.
